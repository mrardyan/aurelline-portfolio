Key decisions in this structure

Remove src/app/ nesting

Vite projects don't need an app/ wrapper. Flat src/ is idiomatic.

Delete src/imports/

Figma export artifacts — renamed or absorbed into proper locations.

Remove all "use client"

Next.js directive — has no effect in Vite.

Switch to @/ imports everywhere

Alias already configured in vite.config.ts — just not used.
